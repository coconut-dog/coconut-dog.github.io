Shopping with Kiokii And...

UX Design

Research

Mobile Design

Kiokii and… is an Asian Lifestyle store that has launched several store locations throughout Ontario. The company also has a website that maintains customers’ online orders. This has allowed for Kiokii to reach markets in-person market as well as online. Despite the company’s increasingly successful business in stores and online, Kiokii is behind its main competitors as they do not yet have a mobile shopping application. According to Mobile eCommerce Stats in 2023, 79% of smartphone users have made purchases online using their mobile devices in the last 6 months. As Kiokii does not yet have a mobile app, there is a high chance that we are missing a large group of potential customers that like to use mobile shopping apps.
